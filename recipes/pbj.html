Peanut Butter and Jelly

Description

What could be better or easier?

It's the pinnacle classic of all foods!

Ingredients

  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly
  • Bread
      Your choice:
    • White
    • Wheat
    • Whole

Steps

  1. Get peanut butter
  2. Place on one piece of bread
  3. Get jelly
  4. Place on other piece of bread
  5. Place one piece of bread on other piece
  6. Voila! You have yourself a scrumptious PB&J!
