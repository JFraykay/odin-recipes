Pizza

Description

No description required for pizza, all mortals know of its presence.

It's pizza!

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Pepperoni
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Flatten dough
  2. Put ingredients on dough
  3. Place in oven
  4. Heat up to 450 degrees Fahrenheit
  5. Leave in for an hour
  6. Take out of oven
  7. BAM! Pizza!
